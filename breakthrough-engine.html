Track Selection (Simulator-Aware)

Each tool/simulator/approach is a separate track with independent metrics. The coordinator selects the highest-quality track using a task-specific quality metric (Q) relative to an independent proxy — not naive averaging.
Based on the v13 consensus-proximity metric: Q_k = (1 / |T_c,track − T_c,proxy|) × √n_crossings × log(1 + n_eff_min)

Track Diagnostic

Poor tracks become diagnostic signals rather than being averaged in. Quality divergence between tracks reveals underlying systematic issues.
